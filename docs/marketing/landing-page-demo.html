Shared Five-Element Space

Western geometric astrology (planetary longitudes, house cusps) and Chinese BaZi (Heavenly Stems, Earthly Branches) are each mapped into the same 5-dimensional Wu-Xing vector space: Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water.

Cosine Similarity Measurement

Angular congruence between the two R⁵ vectors is measured via cosine similarity. A raw coherence score of 1.0 means perfect alignment; 0.0 means orthogonal elemental profiles.

Monte Carlo Calibration

Raw scores are calibrated against Monte Carlo baselines drawn from uniform random birth data. This eliminates false positives caused by structural bias in the element mappings.
